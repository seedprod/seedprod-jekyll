Press Kit

The Press Kit below is for bloggers or anyone else wanting information on SeedProd's Coming Soon Pro plugin for WordPress.

Synopsis

Coming Soon Pro by SeedProd is the quintessential Coming Soon & Maintenance Mode plugin for WordPress

Simple & Flexible

The Coming Soon Pro plugin works with any WordPress Theme and is visible only to non logged in users. This allows you to work on your site while visitors see your coming soon page. Also included is a maintenance mode which allows you to gracefully take your site offline for maintenance after launch.

Easily Capture Leads

The Coming Soon plugin allows you to capture email leads while you build your site. Tight integration with MailChimp, Constant Contact, Aweber, Campaign Monitor, Gravity Forms & more makes this task super easy.

Powerful Features

The Coming Soon Pro Plugin includes features such as Google Font integration, customizable CSS3, White Label Branding, Responsive Mobile Ready, Social Media integration and more. Plus we are constantly developing the plugin.

History

SeedProd's Coming Soon Pro was created with goal of becoming WordPress's quintessential Coming Soon and Maintenance Mode plugin. The initial version launched in the WordPress.org repository back in July of 2011. Since that time the "Ultimate Coming Soon Page" and it's sister plugin "Ultimate Maintenance Mode" have risen to be the top plugins in their class and have amassed over 1,000,000 downloads. A pro version was created in late 2011 with a richer feature set, more active roadmap and development timeline. Coming Soon Pro now has a wide range options and integrations with more being added based on user feedback.
